Anderson Garden

Completed, 2018
LOCATION:
Cincinnati, Ohio


This project, initiated in 2018 for a private residential client in Cincinnati, involved the complete transformation of a steep, underutilized hillside backyard into a programmable, terraced landscape. von Euw Tetrault served as both architect and general contractor, managing all aspects of the design/build process.

The design introduced a series of terraces structured with a mix of hardscape and softscape elements, offering flexibility for everyday use and encouraging indoor-outdoor living. Local fieldstone was used to construct retaining walls, while a thoughtfully curated planting palette supported native wildlife and seasonal interest.

Team members involved include Federica von Euw, Andrew Tetrault.
